Mining the coin.. A rookies' journey

Hi, my name is Dylan im a rookie miner. Im mining for fun, knowledge, and if im lucky, some gold :)

But aren't you too late?

So, I'm obviously quite late coming to this party, that is, anybody interested in getting into Bitcoin mining right now has probably realised that things are quite "difficult" requiring expensive hardware and time in order to find anything!

So, what's the point!?

In truth, knowledge, and pure curiosity! I dont expect to make my fortune doing this, what I want is a new experience that will ultimatey lead to me learning something new. If can achieve that, and I know this sounds cliched, I think that will be payment enough. If I can get an ROI whilst I'm at it, well, even better! :)

What's the deal with this site?

Its a diary, blog, guide etc. I basically want to chronicle everything I've done, and how I've done it during my mining experience. Doing this adds value to this adventure and it will hopefully serve as a guide and provide insite to those that are thinking of jumping on the band wagon.
